## Welcome to SideCar

SideCar is a **local-first** AI coding assistant that transforms your VS Code into a full autonomous agent for coding. It runs on your own machine by default — point it at [Ollama](https://ollama.com) and nothing leaves your laptop. Plug in an Anthropic or OpenAI key when you want frontier-model reasoning, and SideCar will automatically delegate heavy research to a local worker to keep your spend down.

**What you can do with SideCar:**

- **Chat with the agent** — Press `⌘⇧I` (Mac) or `Ctrl+Shift+I` (Windows/Linux) to open the chat panel
- **Inline editing** — Hit `⌘I` (Mac) or `Ctrl+I` (Windows/Linux) to open inline chat
- **Code actions** — Click the 💡 lightbulb (or press `⌘.` / `Ctrl+.`) to access SideCar fixes and explanations
- **Agent modes** — Control the agent's behavior with different modes for file edits, shell commands, and git operations
- **Cost tracking** — See session costs in the status bar
- **Review changes** — Pending changes are shown with file decorations

This walkthrough covers the essentials. Each step takes under a minute — you can come back to it any time from the Command Palette (`SideCar: Open Walkthrough`).
